# Hello World

Every blog starts here. This one is no different.

I've been meaning to write publicly for a while. Notes that live only in Obsidash don't help anyone else, and explaining something in writing forces the kind of clarity that half-remembered thoughts never achieve.

## What I'll write about

Mostly things I'm working through at the time:

- Infrastructure, Kubernetes, and the operational side of software
- TypeScript patterns I find genuinely useful (and ones I wish I hadn't used)
- Notes from reading papers or digging into a library's internals
- Occasionally, non-technical stuff

<Callout variant="tip">
  If a topic shows up here it means I've spent enough time with it to have an opinion worth sharing — not just a summary of the docs.
</Callout>

## What this isn't

A content calendar. I'll write when I have something worth saying.

<Callout variant="warning" title="Caveat">
  Everything here reflects my understanding at the time of writing. Systems change, I change my mind, and the internet is forever — I'll add correction notes rather than quietly editing posts.
</Callout>
